Basic Rules

  1. This Delegation of Spending and Financial Signing Authorities Chart must be read in conjunction with the relevant central agency/organizational regulations, policies, directives and bulletins.
  2. Financial authorities are delegated to positions identified by title.
  3. To exercise financial authority, a person:
    1. must occupy a position listed on this document;
    2. must take the mandatory training; and
    3. must have a valid financial signing authorities card approved by a superior position.
  4. The letter "F "means that the position has been delegated full authorities subject to:
    1. the limitations imposed by legislation and central agency/departmental policies, directives and bulletins, including the Delegation of Spending and Financial Signing Authorities Chart;
    2. the limitations imposed by a superior when authenticating the financial signing authorities card; and
    3. the extent of funds available in the budget allocated to the position.
  5. A blank cell means that no authority has been granted.
  6. Key delegation principles in the Delegation of Spending and Financial Signing Authorities Chart include:
    1. Human resources officers are delegated section 33 of the Financial Administration Act (FAA) only for Pay Items;
    2. No person may exercise more than one of the following authorities for any specific transaction: transaction authority (contracting activity), certification authority (section 34 of the FAA) and payment authority (section 33 of the FAA);
    3. No person shall be permitted to exercise spending or payment authority for a payment from which that person may personally benefit; and
    4. No person may exercise the authorities delegated to a position on an acting basis unless properly designated in writing by an officer to whom the incumbent reports.
  7. A person must only exercise signing authority for the delegated area of authority or responsability centre.
